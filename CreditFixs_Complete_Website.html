How CreditFixs Works

IMPORTANT: CreditFixs is a guidance and consultation service. We provide expert advice and support, but YOU are responsible for filing your own disputes with credit bureaus.

⚠️ Disclaimer: We are NOT a registered financial service, NOT registered with RBI, and NOT an NBFC. We provide guidance only. We cannot guarantee credit score improvement or loan approval. We do NOT file disputes on your behalf - you do, with our guidance and support.

Our Process:

1. Consultation: You tell us about your credit concerns and goals.

2. Guidance: We explain your options and help you understand what can be disputed.

3. Support: We provide templates, sample letters, and step-by-step guidance.

4. Your Action: You file disputes directly with credit bureaus (CIBIL, Equifax, Experian, High Mark).

5. Follow-up: We help you track progress and understand responses from bureaus.
